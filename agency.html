You own 100% of the code, design assets, and intellectual property upon final payment. No licensing fees, no lock-in, no strings attached. We deliver a full Git repository handoff.

We use a simple 50/50 split: 50% upfront to begin work, 50% on delivery. For larger projects, we can structure milestone-based payments. We accept bank transfer, Stripe, and crypto.
